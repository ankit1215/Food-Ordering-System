About Us

Pizza Restaurant started as a single wood-fired oven and a family recipe passed down three generations. Today we still make every dough ball by hand, every morning, in full view of the dining room — because a good pizza has nothing to hide.

We source our tomatoes, herbs and cheese from local growers within a day's drive, and our ovens still burn seasoned oak for that unmistakable char on the crust.

Menu

Explore the wide range of pizza baked in fresh dough and vegetables. Our pizza will tempt all your excitement and senses. Made with a delicate balance of everything cheesy and spicy, you will get to savour the best pizza in town.

  • Pepperoni Classic

    Spiced pepperoni, mozzarella and our slow-simmered tomato base on a hand-tossed crust.

  • Garden Supreme

    Bell peppers, red onion, olives and mushroom on a bed of melted cheese.

  • Rustic Flatbread

    Thin, crackly crust finished with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.

  • Four Cheese

    Mozzarella, gorgonzola, parmesan and provolone, baked until golden and bubbling.

  • Garden Salad

    Crisp seasonal greens, cherry tomatoes and a light herb vinaigrette.

  • House Beverages

    Fresh-pressed juices, iced tea and hand-shaken milkshakes.
